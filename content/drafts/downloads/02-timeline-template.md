## Tips

- List events in the order that best tells your story — oldest to newest usually works best.
- Tie each entry to a document when you can (certificate, letter, record).
- Focus on facts: what happened, when, and what paper proves it.
- Avoid long emotional entries here — save narrative detail for your personal statement.
