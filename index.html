Common questions

What if I don't have anyone helping me?

The guide is written so one person can follow it. Every step is broken down.

What if I can't afford an attorney?

This is built exactly for that situation. It gives you the documents and process without requiring legal representation to start.

What if I already filed once and it was denied?

Many people file more than once. The guide covers how to strengthen a new request and what the court looks for.

Is this only for Connecticut?

Yes — Volume One focuses on Connecticut sentence modification. Other states will be covered in future volumes.
