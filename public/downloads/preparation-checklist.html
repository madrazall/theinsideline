  • I understand sentence modification vs. habeas corpus

  • I know where my motion would be filed

3
Documents to Gather
  • Sentencing order / judgment of conviction
  • Information (charging document) or police report summary
  • Prior motions, appeals, or post-conviction filings
  • Program completion certificates
  • Educational transcripts or GED documentation
  • Vocational training records
  • Employment records
  • Treatment or counseling completion records
  • Institutional conduct / disciplinary history
  • Character reference letters
  • Medical documentation (if relevant)
  • Victim-related correspondence (if applicable)
  • Reentry plan draft
Inside LinePreparation Checklist — continued
4
Organization
  • Dedicated folder or binder
  • Documents sorted into labeled sections
  • Written timeline of key events
  • List of what each document proves
  • Legible copies
  • Pages numbered or tabbed
5
Before Filing
  • Drafted a clear personal statement
  • Double checked names, dates, and case numbers
  • Identified required court forms
  • Understand service / mailing requirements
  • Allowed time for review
  • Considered professional legal assistance
